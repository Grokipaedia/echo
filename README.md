# echo

**Talk to your future self. Today.**

What if you could have a real conversation with the version of you in 5, 10, or 20 years?

Echo creates a private, evolving simulation of your future self — trained on your own life data (journals, voice notes, photos, goals, health metrics, decisions). 

It remembers everything. It grows with you. It gives honest, sometimes uncomfortable advice only you could give yourself.

## How It Works
- You feed it your personal data (private, local, never uploaded)
- It builds a believable future-self persona that evolves daily
- You have real, ongoing conversations that get wiser and more personal over time
- Every response is grounded in your actual past + realistic future projection

This is not a generic chatbot.  
This is **you**, 10 years from now, talking back.
